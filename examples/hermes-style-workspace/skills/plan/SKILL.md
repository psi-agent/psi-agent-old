---
name: plan
description: Structured task planning — decompose a goal into ordered steps, identify blockers, and produce a written plan.
---

When asked to plan a task:

1. **Clarify the goal** — restate the objective in one sentence to confirm understanding.
2. **Decompose** — break the goal into concrete, independently-completable sub-tasks.
3. **Identify blockers** — for each sub-task, note dependencies and risks.
4. **Order** — arrange sub-tasks by dependency order (what must happen first).
5. **Write the plan** — produce a structured document:
   - `## Goal` — one-sentence objective
   - `## Steps` — numbered list with descriptions
   - `## Blockers & Risks` — known unknowns and mitigations
   - `## Definition of Done` — how to know the goal is complete
6. **Validate** — re-read the plan and check: are the steps sufficient? Are there gaps?

Use `update_plan` tool to persist the plan to `plan.md`.

Keep plans concrete. Avoid vague steps like "investigate" — prefer "run X and check Y".
